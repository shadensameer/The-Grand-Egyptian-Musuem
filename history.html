History Of The Grand Egyptian Museum

he Grand Egyptian Museum is the largest archaeological museum in the world, showcasing Egypt’s ancient treasures. Learn about its construction, collections, and milestones.

2002 - Museum Project Begins

The Grand Egyptian Museum project was officially started to house Egypt’s largest collection of artifacts.

2023 - Construction Completed

After years of work, the construction of the museum was completed, making it one of the largest museums in the world.

2025 - Grand Opening

The museum officially opened to the public, showcasing thousands of artifacts including Tutankhamun’s treasures.
